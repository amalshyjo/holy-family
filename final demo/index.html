Welcome to
Holy Family Hospital Muthalakodam

Holy Family Hospital is a Multi-speciality healthcare centre situated at Muthalakodam, in the suburbs of Thodupuzha in Kerala state. It started as a dispensary under St. George Forane Church, Muthalakodam and was handed over to S. H. Jyothi Province Kothamangalam in 1969. It is a religious order under the doctrine of Catholicism with a motto "Merciful Love of Jesus Urges us"- Now it is a rapidly growing 400 bedded multi-speciality hospital with 5 super-specialties and 13 specialties and caring all types of people from all over India as well as abroad. There are 33 dedicated doctors with 18 departments functioning in this hospital. Moreover there are 170 Nurses and 95 paramedical staff are serving this hospital.

Our Departments

There are 18 departments functioning in this hospital. They are General Medicine, General & laparoscopic surgery, Neuro surgery, Urology, Cardiology, Pulmonology, Maxillofacial Surgery, Gastroenterology, Neonatology, Paediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Orthopaedics & Joint Replacement Surgery, Otorhinolaryngology, Dermato - Venereology, Ophthalmology, Dentistry, OAC Child Care, Pain & Palliative Care unit. We have twenty four hours Casualty and Trauma Care Unit with well equipped Theatre complex and intensive care units, computerized laboratory units, Blood Bank, Computerized X-ray, Mammography, Modern 1.5 Tesla MRI, Spiral CT Scan, Color Doppler and Ultrasonography etc.
